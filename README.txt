== Description ==

i-Refer revolutionizes the way online referrals work. Whether you're a merchant looking to expand your brand's reach or an individual eager to earn from referrals, i-Refer provides a seamless platform for connecting, sharing, and profiting from your network.

**Features:**
- Sign up as a merchant or referrer.
- Instant payment for successful referrals.
- Comprehensive referral analytics.
- Single touch referral sharing across social media.
- Trending referrals and product recommendations.

== Installation ==

1. Download the i-Refer plugin from the WordPress plugin repository.
2. Navigate to your WordPress admin panel, go to Plugins > Add New, and choose the downloaded plugin file to upload.
3. Activate i-Refer from your Plugins menu.
4. Visit the i-Refer settings page to configure your preferences.

For detailed setup instructions and configuration, visit our [documentation page](https://i-refer.app/documentation).
